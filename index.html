About Brightsharkes Global Resources

Brightsharkes Global Resources is an integrated services company specializing in general procurement and related services in Nigeria.

Since our incorporation in 2018, we have built a proven track record of excellence in logistics, procurement, community engagement, and social responsibility—particularly serving key players in the oil and gas industry.

Our Services

Professional Procurement

Procurement

We deliver professional, competitive, and timely purchasing, sourcing, warehousing, and supply services. Our procurement scope includes office furniture, fittings, office consumables, confectioneries, household items, appliances, Automotive Gas Oil (AGO), locomotive lubricants, and vehicle spare parts.

Facility Management

Facility Maintenance and Servicing

Managing day-to-day operations that support the core business of any organization or estate/asset. Services include structural facelifts and renovations, housekeeping, cleaning, errands, landscaping, horticulture, beautification, and timely repairs and maintenance by electricians, carpenters, and plumbers.

Logistics & Waste Management

Logistics, Haulage, and Waste Disposal/Management

Ensuring the safe and prompt movement of personnel and materials. Our services cover transportation and cargo services, plus proper handling and environmentally compliant waste disposal.
