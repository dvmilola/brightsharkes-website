About Brightsharkes Global Resources

Brightsharkes Global Resources is your comprehensive business solutions partner, specializing in sourcing, facility services, and personnel recruitment across Nigeria.

We excel in procurement and supply of AGO, lubricants, electrical and solar accessories. Our service portfolio includes facility maintenance, horticulture, general housekeeping, logistics support, and waste management. We also source qualified personnel including cleaners, chefs, laundry handlers, and other skilled professionals.
